```
The angels watch
                    as ravens fly
to empty field
                    the field
my heart
                    dark birds
my friends
                    we lost fellows
the angels watch
                    as we
whisper dreams
        disguise hope
                shout at fear
                        as we mourn the lost
the angels watch
                    as dark birds fly
my fellows gone
                    from empty field
the field my heart
                    I mourn the lost
the angels watch
```
